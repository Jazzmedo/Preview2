In our infant program, we create a nurturing environment where babies are encouraged to explore the world around them through their senses. Guided by the Montessori principle of the Absorbent Mind, infants naturally absorb language, movement, and emotional connections, laying the foundation for future learning. Every detail of the space is thoughtfully designed to support their development, fostering trust, security, and early independence.

Montessori +

Our modernized Montessori program is designed to meet the needs of today’s U.S. educational system while building on the traditional principles of Maria Montessori. Four core elements of such a program are:

Individualized Learning Paths

Palm Grove incorporates modern adaptations through tracking progress to analyze your child’s strengths and struggles, and offering tailored learning experiences that bear in mind what he or she will need moving onward to other learning environments.

Collaborative Learning and Social-Emotional Development

Emphasizing collaborative learning (even in groups larger than 2 which traditional Montessori prefers), for the purpose of developing social-emotional skillsets needed for the K-12 environment.

STEM and Technology Integration

Integrating STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) with hands-on learning while maintaining Montessori’s emphasis on exploration, manipulation, and self-discovery, using modern technology (tablets, games, and robotics kits).

Cultural Awareness and Global Citizenship

In response to global interconnectedness, we emphasize cultural awareness, diversity, and global citizenship. Our themes include units on global sustainability, environmental stewardship, and international cooperation, with an emphasis on understanding the interconnectedness of people and the planet.
