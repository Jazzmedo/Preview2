Our Montessori Curriculum

Our school is far from traditional, but also very Classical Montessori. It embraces the Montessori Prepared Environment, that tailors the learning environment to support individual learning at the child's own pace. It also exposes children to designated group learning sessions, that mimic that of the public schooling system, to enable them to develop the skill sets necessary for learning in that environment.


Across the board, we are revamping Parkwood Montessori into our first Palm Grove Academy, to incorporate exciting new ways to embrace Montessori. We are equipping our young learners with the tools they will need to succeed beyond Montessori.

Our Facilities

The Prepared Environment

Our Montessori Learning Environment is prepared to meet your child where he/she is. Lesson plans are displayed in the form of manipulatives in individualized trays or baskets, with a learning objective. The teacher's role is as a guide to see them through each activity successfully.

Swimming Classes

Having an on-site swimming pool enhances the learning experience by offering a unique opportunity for physical development and water safety skills. With both school-year and summer swimming classes, children can build confidence in the water, improve coordination, and enjoy an active, fun environment all year round.

Multi Age Learning Areas

Our Montessori learning zones are thoughtfully designed to encourage interaction across age groups, fostering a collaborative environment where children learn from and support each other. By working alongside peers of varying ages, children develop social skills, empathy, and leadership abilities.

Our Beautiful Playground

Our facility offers a vibrant, outdoor space where children can explore, play, and develop essential physical and social skills. Designed with natural elements and ample room for creative play, it encourages children to engage with their surroundings, fostering a love for nature and active outdoor exploration.

Qualified Educators

Our certified Montessori teachers are passionate about fostering each child's unique potential, creating a nurturing environment that supports individual learning styles. With a focus on hands-on exploration and guided discovery, our educators provide personalized attention to help children develop at their own pace, building a strong foundation for lifelong learning and growth.
